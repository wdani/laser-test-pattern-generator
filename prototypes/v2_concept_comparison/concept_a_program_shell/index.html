T2

Hybrid Planner

Mock operation plan for combining CNC cleanup with laser marking and cutting.

CNC firstLaser details secondKeep origin pinCut outline last
01CNC pocket2.0 mm depth, 3.175 mm endmill, 18 min
02Manual handoffKeep origin pin, vacuum chips, refocus laser
03Laser engraveOffset fill, 3000 mm/min, 12% power, 9 min
04Laser cut outlineNC output profile, verify S scale, 5 min
Why this order?

Mill the shallow pocket before engraving so the laser darkens the final surface. Cut the outside last so the coaster does not shift.
